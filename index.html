1. Information We Collect

1.1 Personal Information

User Identifiers: When you use the App, we may assign a unique user ID. This ID helps us manage your subscriptions, maintain service continuity, and understand usage patterns without directly linking activities to your real identity.

Subscription and Purchase Information: If you subscribe to our premium services (including automatic weekly renewals), we collect information related to your subscription status and purchase history. This may include subscription type, duration, renewal status, transaction identifiers, and payment confirmation records from our subscription service provider, RevenueCat.

1.2 Usage Information

Analytics Data (Amplitude): We use Amplitude, a third-party analytics service, to understand how you interact with the App. Data includes screens viewed, buttons pressed, and duration of feature usage. We use this information solely to improve the App's functionality, design, and performance.

Prompt and Image Generation: When you use the App to generate images, your text prompts, customization settings, and related generation parameters are processed through AI models provided by third-party services including:

  • Stability.ai
  • Replicate
  • OpenRouter
These services are facilitated through AiProxy.
Text Prompt Handling: Your text prompts and generation parameters are transmitted securely to the external services for processing.
Storage of Prompts and Generated Images: We may temporarily store your prompts and generated images to provide the service, improve functionality, and maintain your generation history within the app. Your prompt library is stored locally on your device.
Reference Images: If you upload reference images to guide the generation process, these images are transmitted to the AI services but are not permanently stored on our servers.

1.3 User-Generated Content

Saved Prompts: The App allows you to save and manage your prompt library for future use. These are stored locally on your device and are accessible through the Prompt Library feature.

Generated Images: Images created using the App are primarily stored on your device. You may choose to save, share, or export these images according to your preferences.

1.4 Device and Technical Information

Device Data: We may collect device model, operating system version, language settings, and unique identifiers.

Log Data: Our servers may automatically record certain information ("Log Data") such as IP addresses, App version, request times/dates, and general usage patterns.
